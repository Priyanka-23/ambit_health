General Consultation
A general consultation at Ambit Health for home health care involves assessing the patient’s medical history and current conditions to determine the level of care required. The provider explains available services, such as skilled nursing or therapy, and develops a personalized care plan. Family involvement and caregiver training are discussed, along with necessary medical equipment and potential home modifications. Insurance and payment options are reviewed to outline coverage and costs. Finally, the care plan is coordinated with the patient’s primary physician for continuity and adjusted as needed based on progress.
Basic Medical Equipment’s
Ambit Health provides a wide range of essential medical equipment to support patients in the comfort of their homes. This includes mobility aids like walkers, wheelchairs, and canes for enhanced movement and independence. Respiratory equipment such as oxygen tanks and CPAP machines are available for patients with breathing issues. We offer hospital beds with adjustable settings, as well as pressure-relief mattresses to prevent bedsores. Ambit also supplies infusion pumps, IV stands, and wound care equipment for patients with specialized medical needs. All equipment is delivered and set up at home, with full instructions for safe use.
Diagnostic Servicess
Diagnostic services at Ambit Health involves assessing your medical history and current health concerns to determine which tests or procedures are required. The provider explains available diagnostic services, such as blood tests, imaging, or EKGs, and how they can be performed at home. They ensure you are comfortable with the procedures and discuss the timing and frequency of services. Any necessary equipment is brought to your home, and the results are sent to your physician for analysis. Insurance and payment options are reviewed to clarify coverage, ensuring seamless care without leaving your home.
Pharmacy Servicess
Ambit Health’s in-home pharmacy delivery services starts with an assessment of the patient’s medication needs, including current prescriptions and any special requirements. The provider explains the process for setting up home delivery, including scheduling and medication management. They discuss potential packaging options, such as pre-sorted doses, to ensure ease of use. Insurance coverage for medications is reviewed, along with any out-of-pocket costs. The service also includes coordination with the patient’s healthcare providers to manage prescriptions, refills, and ensure timely deliveries, all customized to meet the patient's ongoing needs.
Physiotherapy Services
Ambit Health’s iin-home physiotherapy services involves assessing the patient’s physical condition and medical history to determine specific rehabilitation needs. The provider explains available therapies, such as mobility exercises, strength training, or post-surgical rehabilitation, and develops a personalized treatment plan. Family involvement may be discussed to assist with exercises between sessions, along with any necessary home modifications to support mobility. Medical equipment, like walkers or exercise tools, may be recommended. Insurance and payment options are reviewed to outline coverage. The plan is coordinated with the patient's physician and adjusted as recovery progresses.
Vaccination
vaccination involves reviewing the patient’s medical history and current vaccination status. The healthcare provider explains the recommended vaccines based on age, health conditions, and travel plans. They discuss the benefits, possible side effects, and any precautions to take post-vaccination. The patient is informed about the timing and scheduling of required doses, especially for vaccines requiring multiple shots. Insurance and payment options are reviewed to determine coverage. Finally, the consultation ensures the patient understands when to return for follow-ups or boosters, ensuring proper immunization tracking.
